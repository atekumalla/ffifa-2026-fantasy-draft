Points are awarded based on match results and goals scored.

⚠️ Knockout Rules:
• There is no draw in knockout rounds — there's always a winner.
• The team that advances (even via penalties) gets the full 3 win points. The losing team gets 0.
• Penalty shootout goals do NOT earn goal points — only regular & extra time goals count at 0.75 pts each.

📋 Example: Germany 1–1 Paraguay (pens 4–2):
• Germany: 1 goal × 0.75 + 3 (win) = 3.75 pts
• Paraguay: 1 goal × 0.75 + 0 (loss) = 0.75 pts

✅ Note: Goals conceded do not result in negative points.
